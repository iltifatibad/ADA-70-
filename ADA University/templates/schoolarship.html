Schoolarship

Students, doctoral students, and school students studying in the fields of Astronomy, Chemistry, Chemical Engineering, Biology, Physics, Mathematics, Geography, Geology, Aerospace Engineering, and Cosmology who obtain membership can earn a monthly Scholarship of $60 from the society. those who do will continue to receive the Scholarship for as long as their membership continues. Although 200 people will get the chance of Scholarship in the initial phase, but this number will continue to increase by 200 people in the next registration period compared to the previous registration period. Bachelor's, Master's students, doctoral students with a grade average of at least 80-82 (2.7GPA) studying in any of the mentioned technical fields, especially students with high annual grades, especially technical subjects, can apply for the Scholarship. The registration process lasts for 3 months and then the names of the winners are announced. Thus, the scholarship registration process is announced 4 times a year. This mechanism creates a chance for those who lost the chance to win the Scholarship during the first process to win this chance again the next time. The activity that the members will show during 3 months and also their continuous membership in the society can help even those who lost this chance during the first election to win the next elections.
